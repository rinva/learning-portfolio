# Airtable Admin Certification

**Completed:** June 2025  
**Platform:** Airtable Academy

This certification provided in-depth training on managing Airtable at the enterprise level. Topics covered included:

- Admin panel features and settings
- User and group management
- Managing workspaces, interfaces, and bases
- Security, permissions, and identity integration (SSO/SCIM)
- Reporting and global settings
- Dataset verification and publishing

This was a structured, professional certification that deepened my understanding of enterprise Airtable use and organizational governance.
